About Us

Established in 1995, ISCO Intersec Security is the leading provider of security services in Rwanda. We provide forward thinking solutions that have been streamlined and digitized for efficient and timely operational standards.

Recently rebranded from Intersec Security to ISCO Intersec Security, we have transformed from a traditional security company to an innovative service provider in services that require our expertise & vast national network including :-

  •  Guarding services

  •  Video Surveillance, Alarms & Access Control

  •  Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting Equipment


  •  Cash-in-Transit & Cash Processing Services

  •  Courier & Logistics Services

  •  4G Enabled Security Services




A new state of the art Cash Processing Center as well as a new Headquarters base are currently under construction and will be ready by end of 2015.
ISCO Intersec Security is ISO certified - ISO 9001: 2008

What has changed at ISCO Intersec Security?

Our Vision

“To be the preferred security provider in the region with the most reliable security services.”
We are working towards our vision with innovative solutions for today’s fast paced world whilst also developing and empowering our employees to further serve clients better.

Our People

We recognise that our people are key to the successful delivery of the new ISCO Intersec Security experience and have thus invested in indentifying the best talent, developing their skill set and creating a conducive environment to grow them into effective leaders. A restructured system is taking employees through a vigorous training program that covers security systems, security management, disaster response, customer service and administrative skills.

Our Systems

  • All systems have been streamlined & digitised for efficient & timely operational standards.
  • We have introduced a centralised customer service line that handles any query from billing to quotes - 3031.
  • We have established customer feedback channels through a dedicated email address (feedback@isco.co.rw) as well as our twitter handle (@iscosecurity) and facebook page (iscosecurity).
  • We have a fully trained key account management team that will assess risk, recommend security solutions and manage your account on a daily basis.

Added Value

  • We now offer Courier, Logistics, Transport & Warehousing services.
  • We have established 4G Security Services offering 4G Internet connectivity for businesses and homes as well as 4G enabled security systems such as IP video surveillance cameras.
  • A cash processing center is under construction to expand our CIT services to a full service cash solution including Cash sorting / inspection / packaging, ATM replinishment as well as Vault solutions.
  • A team of security specialists has been trained to provide 360 degree security solutions that go past guarding services to include security consultancy right through to crisis management advisory.
